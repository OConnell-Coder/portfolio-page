About Me

Growing up, I was always told that I "think like a robot." I suppose I took that to heart (or should I say to my PSU) because I decided to learn how to talk to computers. I learned full-stack developer skills from the University of Pennsylvania's LPS Coding Bootcamp. At first, it was a drastic change from my previous careers as an English Professor and a Corporate Accountant. But I quickly found that both careers played a foundational role to my understanding of coding. From English, I brought creativity, a perpensity for syntax and grammar, and a constant conceptualization of how form and design informs function and communication. From Accounting, I brought efficiency, accuracy, and precision, in a field where attention-to-detail is equally critical.
